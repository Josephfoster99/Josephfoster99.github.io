About TechieLink

Techie Link is an IT company established in 2021. Techielink has traversed an enriching journey of more than a year and has emerged as one of the largest IT Systems Support provider, solar & electric Installation, supporting customers from small and medium businesses and also providing a specialist service for the educational institutes. Techielink has extensive experience in delivering high-quality Solar & electric installation and IT Infrastructure services for all kinds of verticals and technology environments. Driven by a customer oriented approach with focus on absolute standards of operational excellence, each experience has made us develop more innovative solutions and has enabled us to understand business, technology and operational elements in great detail. We want to empower our customers to be able to reach their goals easily and live a more fulfilling and productive life. Our highly committed and skilled set of professionals will add value by proposing technically superior and commercially competitive solutions.
